I am a researcher and educator specializing in fluid mechanics, computational fluid dynamics, energy storage, nanofluids, and renewable energy. My work integrates mathematical physics, materials science, and advanced numerical simulations to address real-world challenges in energy sustainability, with a focus on net-zero goals and Saudi Vision 2030.

With international experience across Saudi Arabia, China, Lebanon, and Pakistan, I have led research on nano-enhanced phase change materials, battery thermal management, and AI-powered grid integration. I also serve on editorial boards of leading scientific journals, and am passionate about mentorship, research collaboration, and science outreach.
